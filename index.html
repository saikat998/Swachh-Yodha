Training Guide

👋 Welcome to the Team!

You are a Swachh Yoddha, and your work is one of the most important jobs in our city. You are on the front line of public health and environmental protection. This guide will help you perform your duties safely, professionally, and efficiently.

Module 1: Daily Preparation

  • Check Your Gear (PPE): Your safety is #1. Always wear your 🧤 Gloves, 🥾 Safety Boots, 😷 Face Mask, and 🧥 High-Visibility Jacket.
  • Inspect Your Vehicle: Do a quick check of your vehicle. Ensure it's clean and all tools are present.
  • Check the App: Log in to the Swachh Yoddha app. Review your assigned route and any special instructions for the day.

Module 2: The Collection Process

  • Be Professional: Handle bins carefully to avoid spills. If anything spills, clean it up immediately. Be courteous to all residents.
  • Handle Waste Correctly: Separate waste properly. 🔵 Dry Waste (Paper, plastic), 🟢 Wet Waste (Food scraps), and 🔴 Hazardous Waste (Batteries, glass) must be kept separate.
  • Update the App: After collection from a house, mark the status as "Picked Up" or "Not Picked Up" in the app immediately.

Module 3: Health & Safety

  • Proper Lifting: Bend your knees, not your back! Lift with your legs to avoid injury. [Image of correct lifting posture]
  • Wash Your Hands: Wash hands thoroughly with soap after your shift and before eating.
  • Report Injuries: Report any cut or injury to your supervisor right away, no matter how small.

Module 4: End of Shift

  • Dispose Correctly: Unload waste into the correct sections at the municipal disposal center.
  • Clean Your Vehicle: Wash your vehicle so it's ready for the next day.
  • Final Report: Mark your route as "Complete" in the app. Report any major issues you noticed during your route.

Thank you for building a cleaner, healthier India!
